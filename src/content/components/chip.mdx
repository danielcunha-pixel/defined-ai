Chips are compact labels used for tags, filters, or selected values.

## When to use

- To display selected filters or tags
- As compact, non-primary action labels

The Chip component supports seven color variants plus enabled/hover/pressed/focus visual states and click interaction. **Tooltip on hover is not part of the Chip** and must not be implemented on the component itself.
